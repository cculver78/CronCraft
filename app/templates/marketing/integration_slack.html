Slack Alerts for Cron & Scheduled Jobs

CronCraft sends late and failed job notifications to Slack via Incoming Webhooks on Pro and Team hosted plans.

Email alerts are included on Free — upgrade when you want #incidents style visibility.

Setup (high level)

  1. Create a Slack incoming webhook in your workspace.
  2. Paste the webhook URL into CronCraft integration settings.
  3. Choose which jobs or teams should notify the channel.

Exact UI labels live in the app after you register. Technical API details: GitHub.
